Sensors

Sensors allow you to measure your skill in a paricular sport for example. A golf ball has a sensor in it that allows you to see how far you hit the ball and how fast you hit it. This is a great way to improve your game and see how far you can hit the ball. This is but one use case for one sport. There are many other kind of sensors that can be used in many different sports. With the data these sensors collect you can improve in the sport you are playing. order here!

Wearable Technology

Werable technology allows you to measure a lot of data involving you fitness. This data can be used to improve your fitness and help you reach your goals. Wearable technology can be used in many different sports. For example, a football player can use wearable technology to see how fast they are running and how many steps they take in a game. This can be used to improve their fitness and help them reach their goals. An example of wearable technology is the Apple watch. order here!

VAR Technology

VAR is short for video assistant referee. With VAR technology the referee in football have a more accurate view and understanding of what happened and so they can make better decisions on the field. order here!
